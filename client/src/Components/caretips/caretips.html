Expert Pet Care Guide & Tips

Everything you need to know about caring for your furry friends. From basic care to emergency situations, we've got you covered.

Basic Pet Hygiene

  • Regular grooming and brushing (2-3 times per week)
  • Dental care and teeth brushing
  • Nail trimming every 2-4 weeks
  • Regular baths with pet-safe shampoo

Feeding Guidelines

  • Puppies: 3-4 times daily
  • Adult dogs: 2 times daily
  • Fresh water available 24/7
  • Portion size based on weight and activity level

Frequently Asked Questions

Regular check-ups are recommended every 6-12 months for adult pets, and more frequently for puppies, kittens, and senior pets. Vaccinations and preventive care should be maintained according to your vet's recommendations.

Watch for difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, seizures, collapse, severe vomiting or diarrhea, inability to urinate, and signs of extreme pain. When in doubt, contact your veterinarian immediately.
